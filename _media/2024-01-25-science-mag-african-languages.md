*Science Magazine* featured **Dr. Shamsuddeen Muhammad** among African researchers tackling linguistic inequity in artificial intelligence.  
The article discusses how **HausaNLP** and related community projects are building datasets and benchmarks to ensure **African linguistic diversity is represented in modern AI systems**, challenging the dominance of English-centric models.
