We are excited to announce the release of our paper: **AfriHate: A Multilingual Collection of Hate Speech and Abusive Language Datasets for African Languages**.

The paper introduces a comprehensive dataset aimed at advancing research on hate speech and abusive language detection in African languages.

Read the paper here: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.05602](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.05602)
